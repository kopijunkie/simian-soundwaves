Snow Patrol

Band of the Month | November 2009

Posted by Pinky
Filed under: Features


Band Info


  • Country of Origin: Northern Ireland/Scotland (formed in Dundee, Scotland)
  • Years Active: 1994 - Present
  • Genre: Alternative Rock, Power Pop, Indie Rock
  • Website: www.snowpatrol.com
  • Nonsensical Fun Facts:
  • All of their albums' booklet end off with the line "Mums and Dads of the world be patient with your children".
  • Following the success of Final Straw, Snow Patrol were nominated for Best Newcomers at the Q Awards in 2004...the band had been around for 10 years already by then and that was their third album.

  • Paul Wilson – bass guitar, backing vocals

  • Tom Simpson – keyboards, samples
